FAQ & Terms

What is Amapiano?
A modern South African house style with deep “log drum” bass and smooth keys.
What is Mbira?
A thumb piano from Zimbabwe used for ceremony and storytelling.
Isicathamiya
All-male, close-harmony a cappella; the name means “to tread softly”.
