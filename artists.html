Ladysmith Black Mambazo

Carrying Isicathamiya (pronounced ee-see-kah-tah-MEE-yah) with tight a-cappella arrangements.

The word means to walk softly or tread carefully because singers move with gentle dance steps while performing.

It’s sung by all-male choirs, usually in rich vocal harmonies.
