Genres

Amapiano

Piano-led house with deep “log drum” basslines; smooth, lounge-to-dance energy.

Kwaito

90s township house at mid-tempo; catchy chants, swagger, and street style.

Gqom

Minimal, percussive club music from Durban. Raw, hypnotic rhythm for the floor.

Mbira

Shona lamellophone (thumb piano) music from Zimbabwe. Layered, cyclical melodies.

Isicathamiya (Mbube)

Close-harmony a cappella choral tradition. Soft-stepping, precise, powerful.

Sungura

Fast, guitar-driven dance music. Bright leads and energetic rhythms.
